DAY 1

Arrival & 5th Avenue Vibes

Settle into your family resort in Playa del Carmen. In the evening, stroll down the vibrant "Quinta Avenida" and visit the beachside Parque Fundadores.

celebration
Must See: Parque Fundadores Watch the "Voladores de Papantla" (flying pole dancers) the kids will be mesmerized!
restaurant
Dinner: La Cueva del Chango A jungle-garden setting that feels magical. Very kid-friendly with authentic chilaquiles and fresh juices.

DAY 2

The Ultimate Eco-Park: Xcaret

A full day at Mexico's "Disneyland of Nature." It's huge, so plan to arrive early! Don't miss the spectacular night show detailing Mexico's history.

water_drop
Underground Rivers Float through caves in crystal clear water. Life jackets are mandatory and safe for the 5-year-old (who can also be towed by dad!).
flutter_dash
Wildlife Encounters Visit the massive Butterfly Pavilion and the Jaguar Island.
Age 5 "Children's World" Area
Age 10 Snorkeling inlet
Age 13 Adventure trails
DAY 3

Ancient Ruins & Cool Cenotes

Start early (8 AM) to beat the heat at the Tulum Ruins, perched on a cliff. Then, cool off in a nearby freshwater Cenote.

landscape
Tulum Ruins Take the "train" (tractor wagon) from the parking lot to save little legs. Look for iguanas sunning on the rocks!
pool
Cool Off: Cenote Azul An open-air cenote perfect for families. It has shallow areas for the 5yo and a "cliff" jump for the 13yo. See Cenote Photo

Age 10 Spotting Turtles
